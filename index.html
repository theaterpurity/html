Definition of Purity Test:

"This is a self-graded test to see how pure you are when it comes to the theater world" - Wikipedia


About the theater Purity Test

Unlike the traditional purity test, the theater Purity Test scores the most pure a 0/100 and the least pure a 100/100. Simply put, the theater Purity Test tells you how many checkboxes you clicked. Many of the questions are common to other purity test. However, the theater Purity test uses questions more taylored to high school and college students. Additionally, we added a few fun questions inbetween.


Instructions:

Simply click on the checkboxes that apply to you. At the end submit your responce and get a score. As you grow up, take the test again and compare your score. None of the topics in this test are supported or recommended. This is merely a way to illustrate your purity.
